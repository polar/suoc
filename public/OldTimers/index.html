The SUOC Oldtimers' Phone List

Congratulations!

You have made it to the Oldtimers list! This list is private. There are no public links to this list so that it cannot be entered by web searchers, and other bogons of moral turpitude. Feel free to put the link on your hotlist or bookmarks, but please do NOT put it on publicly accessible home pages. Please respect the privacy of your friends.

You have just commited the access to this page since Feb 4, 1996.

This page is brought to you by the courtesy and not so hard work of Polar, a lover of people, the outdoors, and good food.

The regular SUOC web page is http://suoc.syr.edu, which is always good to see what the club is up to these days.

SUOC Alumni List Server

We have our own E-mail list server, which is suocalumni@listserv.syr.edu. You may send messages to that list only if you are a subscribed member of that list. Furthermore, you must only send messages from the email address with which you subscribed.

You may subscribe and unsubscribe by pointing your browser at:

http://listserv.syr.edu

There you will find instructions on subscribing and unsubscribing to a "listserv" list. The name of our alumni list is called "suocalumni".

For fun there are other lists that you may subscribe to:

suocalumni
This list is for the SUOC alumni. Hopefully low traffic, except for Becca Kent, who likes to post the same message three or four times. heh heh. :)
suoc
This list is for the SUOC general public and what the current students are up to, and what is currently happening in the club. Things like potlucks and bike trips along with other general shananagans get posted.
caving
This list is for the SUOC caving people, since somehow they seem to think themselves somewhat different than other people.
suocsummer
This list was created for students and such that are around Syracuse for the summer.

Mistakes and Corrections

If you notice any mistakes, omittions, or just things plain wrong and out of date please send me, Polar, an Email message at Polar <polar@syr.edu> so that I may correct it.
