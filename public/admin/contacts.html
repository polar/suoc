The best way to find out about the club is to come to one of our weekly meetings. You can also visit us up at the Equipment Room.

If you can't make the meetings or the E-Room times, you can call one of the Club Officers to find out how to join. Or check out the list of trips going out this week, and if you find one you like, call the trip leader to see if you can go.
